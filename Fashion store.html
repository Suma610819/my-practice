Hautr couture

What is Hautr couture?

Haute couture (French for 'high sewing' or 'high dressmaking';
IPA: [ˌoːt kuˈtyʁ]) refers to the creation of exclusive fashions.
It is a common term for custom-fitted clothing as produced primarily in Paris
but also in other fashion capitals such as New York, London, and Milan.

Benifits of Hautr Couture?

  • Haute couture is known for its legacy of being meticulous, detail-oriented, handcrafted, and perfect to a point of no-fault designs that often stand to become unforgettable masterpieces.
  • From hand-sewn embellishments to intricate embroidery, Haute Couture exemplifies the highest standards of skill and precision.
  • Haute couture is not only made-to-order for a specific customer, it is usually made from high-quality, expensive fabric and sewn with extreme attention to detail and finish, often using time-consuming hand-executed techniques.

Ready-to-wear

Ready-to-wear is the term used for clothing that's pre-manufactured to standard sizes and
sold in finished condition. For this reason,
it differs from made-to-measure and bespoke clothing. However,
it is different to the mass-market clothing you'll find in high-street retailers.

Econamy fashion

What is economy fashion?

  • In a circular economy for fashion, clothes are used more often, which allows their value to be captured fully.
  • Once clothes cannot be used anymore, recycling them into new clothes allows the value of the materials to be captured at different levels.
  • Circular fashion refers to an approach within the fashion industry that aims to create a closed-loop system, reducing waste and maximizing the lifespan of clothing and textiles.

Fast fashion

Fast fashion is the business model of replicating recent catwalk trends and high-fashion designs,
mass-producing them at a low cost, and bringing them to retail quickly while demand
is at its highest. The term fast fashion is also used generically
to describe the products of this business model.
